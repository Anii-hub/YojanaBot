About YojanaBot

YojanaBot is a free AI-powered tool that helps every Indian citizen discover government schemes they actually qualify for — in English or Hindi.

🇮🇳

What is YojanaBot?

India has hundreds of central and state government schemes — for farmers, students, women, senior citizens, differently abled persons, workers, and more. Most people never find out which ones they qualify for because the information is scattered across dozens of websites and PDFs.

YojanaBot solves this. Just describe yourself in plain text — your state, age, income, caste, and occupation — and the tool instantly searches through scheme documents to show you only the schemes you are personally eligible for, with the exact benefit amount and a direct link to apply.

How to Use YojanaBot — 3 Simple Steps

1

Describe Yourself

Write a short description of yourself in English or Hindi — mention your state, age, income, caste, and occupation. A few sentences is all it takes.

2

Choose Your Language

Select English or Hindi. All results — scheme names, benefits, and eligibility details — will be shown in your chosen language.

3

View & Apply

See your matched schemes ranked by relevance. Click Apply Now to go directly to the official government portal.

📋

Documents to Keep Ready When Applying

Most government schemes require a common set of documents. Having these ready will speed up your application:

Aadhaar Card
Bank Passbook (with IFSC)
Income Certificate
Caste Certificate (if applicable)
Age Proof (Birth / School Certificate)
Ration Card
Passport-size Photographs
Land Records (for farmer schemes)
Disability Certificate (for Divyang schemes)
Domicile / Residence Certificate
